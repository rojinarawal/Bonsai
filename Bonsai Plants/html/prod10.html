Product Details

Cherry blossom bonsai is a deciduous species prized for its delicate, fragrant flowers and beautiful, smooth bark. This type of bonsai is native to Asia and is a symbol of spring in many cultures. Cherry blossom bonsai prefer bright, indirect light and well-drained soil, and should be protected from strong winds and extreme temperatures. Regular watering, pruning, and fertilization will help keep your cherry blossom bonsai healthy and blooming. Cherry blossom bonsai is a stunning addition to any bonsai collection, adding beauty and grace to your indoor or outdoor space.
