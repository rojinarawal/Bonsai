Product Details

Bald Cypress bonsai is a deciduous species prized for its unique, needle-like leaves and distinctive, cypress "knees". This type of bonsai is native to the wetlands of the southeastern United States and is known for its tolerance of wet soil and high humidity. Bald Cypress bonsai prefer bright, indirect light and well-drained soil, and should be protected from strong winds and extreme temperatures. Regular watering, pruning, and fertilization will help keep your Bald Cypress bonsai healthy and thriving. Bald Cypress bonsai is a beautiful and unusual addition to any bonsai collection, adding a touch of the swamp to your indoor or outdoor space.
