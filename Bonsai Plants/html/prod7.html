Product Details

Ficus bonsai is a popular species known for its tolerance of a wide range of growing conditions and ease of care. This type of bonsai has attractive, glossy leaves and aerial roots that can be trained to add visual interest. Ficus bonsai prefer bright, indirect light and well-drained soil, and should be protected from direct sun and sudden temperature changes. Regular watering, pruning, and fertilization are essential to keep your ficus bonsai healthy and thriving.
