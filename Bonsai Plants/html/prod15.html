Product Details

Japanese Black Pine bonsai is a coniferous species prized for its long, dark green needles and rugged, windswept appearance. This type of bonsai is native to Japan and is known for its tolerance of salt spray and strong winds. Japanese Black Pine bonsai prefer bright, indirect light and well-drained soil, and should be protected from extreme temperatures. Regular watering, pruning, and fertilization will help keep your Japanese Black Pine bonsai healthy and robust. Japanese Black Pine bonsai is a classic and timeless addition to any bonsai collection, adding strength and elegance to your indoor or outdoor space.
